# Rural Health Equity in Wales

## Project Overview
This project analyses rural health equity across Wales using geospatial 
visualisation and data science methods. It was produced as part of a 
10-week professional placement with Rural Health Compass (RHC).

## Placement Details
- **Student:** Nina Akporiaye
- **Organisation:** Rural Health Compass (RHC)
- **Supervisor:** Dr Veronika Rasic
- **University:** University of Aberdeen, MSc Health Data Science
- **Period:** June – August 2026

## Project Themes
- Theme 1: Access to Care
- Theme 2: Health Outcomes  
- Theme 3: Social Determinants of Health

## Data Sources
- WIMD 2019: Welsh Government (Open Government Licence v3.0)
- ONS LSOA Shapefiles: Office for National Statistics (Open Government Licence v3.0)
- ONS Rural/Urban Classification: Office for National Statistics (Open Government Licence v3.0)
